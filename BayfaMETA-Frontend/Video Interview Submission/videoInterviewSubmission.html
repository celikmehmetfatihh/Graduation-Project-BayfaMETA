Video Interview Submission

Please follow these guidelines to ensure a successful video interview:

  • Understand the topic and research as needed before recording your response.
  • Choose a quiet, well-lit space for recording your video.
  • Dress professionally as you would for an in-person interview.
  • Keep your response focused and to the point.
  • Speak clearly and naturally.
  • Practice speaking about the topic before recording if possible.
  • Take advantage of unlimited attempts if offered.
  • Review your response before submitting.
